Beef Recipe

Below is a guide to prepare beef to be taken with the chapatis and potatoes

Once the chapatis are ready, you will need stew, abd what better stew than use potatoes and beef.

Ingridients

  • 1 kg stewing beef, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper (red, green, or yellow), chopped (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Brown & Sauté:

    2. Brown beef in batches. Sauté onion in same pot until softened. Add tomatoes, bell pepper (optional), garlic, ginger, cook further.

  2. Spice & Simmer:

    3. Add spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, chili powder), cook briefly. Pour in broth and water, scrape browned bits. Return beef, simmer covered for 1-1.5 hours.

  3. Season & Serve

    4. Season with salt, garnish with cilantro. Serve hot with chapati.
