Tomato Recipe

Below is a guide to prepare Kenyan Potatoes to be taken with the chapatis

Once the chapatis are ready, you will need stew, abd what better stew than use potatoes and beef.

Ingridients

  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Boil & Spice

    2. Boil cubed potatoes. Saut√© onion in oil, add ginger-garlic paste & spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder), cook briefly.

  2. Simmer & Thicken

    3. Add diced tomatoes (undrained) to the pan with spices, simmer. Add cooked potatoes and water, simmer until sauce thickens.

  3. Season & Serve

    4. Season with salt, garnish with cilantro. Serve hot with chapati.
